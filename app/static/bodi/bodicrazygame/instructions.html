Instructions
  • Use 'a' and 'd' to move.
  • Press the space bar to jump.
  • Move and click your mouse to shoot and aim.
  • Collect coins and metal when you kill robots.
  • GAME OVER: is when you turn invisible and your score stops going up.
  • Health can be re-gained, when you buy health hit 'q' up to five times to rebuild your health.
  • When you get 50 coins press 2 to buy a laser and press 3 to arm the laser.
  • When you have 15 metal press 'n' to deploy a nuke. Be prepared for the insanity that follows. Some choose not to use the nuke.
  • You can exchange metal for coins by pressing 4 and coins for metal by pressing 5, but you lose each trade.
  • SHOPPING
    • When you come upon the shop, click on one of the icons you select to buy (There is a bug, don't click the other shop)
    • On the right is jump boost 5 coins
    • In the middle is health 10 coins
    On the left is a power up 5 coins (I think)
  • HINTS
    • To speed up the game, stay near the beginning moving back and forth to have the robots come to you.
    • If you see no robots for a long time, you can start to travel.
    • If you want to shop, you must travel until you find a shop.
